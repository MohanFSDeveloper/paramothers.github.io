Sheet 6: Property Keys
1 log4j.threshold = [level]



2 log4j.appender.[appenderName]=[FQN of class] define the appender first while writing configuration file.


log4j.appender.[appenderName].[options]=[value]



3 log4j.appender.[appenderName].layout =[FQN of class]



4 log4j.rootLogger = [level],[appender1, appender2,…] root logger should be first for inheritance of level



5 log4j.logger.[loggerName(usually package name)]=[level],[appdender1, appender2] non-root logger should be declared after root logger



6 log4j.additivity.[loggerName] = [true/false]



7 log4j.renderer.[FQN of domainobject] = [FQN of Renderer of a domainObject]
